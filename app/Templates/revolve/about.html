About Us


Hello and Welcome to the MAKTAB blog.

This blog was created by the group 2 of MAKTAB 89. The members of this group are:

  • Farhad Faghih
  • Sayyed Mohammad Razavi
  • Amin Najari
  • Nima Abarghoie
  • Hadi Heidari
  • Zahra Rezaei

The pages of this blog are divided into two general categories:

1- Public pages: including:

  • About-us, the page you are on
  • Contact-us, A page through which you can communicate with us
  • Home page, where you can see the last three posted posts
  • Posts page, where you can see all the posted posts
  • and A special page for each post, where in addition to viewing each post entirety along with the author's name and approved comments, you can also leave a comment (if you logged in). These comments will be displayed after management approval.

2- Dedicated pages: including pages for authors and management.

Enjoy and share your comments and suggestions with us through the form on the contact page.
